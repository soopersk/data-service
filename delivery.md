# Q2 / H1 2026 Achievements & Q3 Priorities
### Delivery Stream / Pod — Leadership Review

---

## Executive Summary

H1 2026 marked a decisive shift from manual, reactive operations to an automated, intelligence-driven platform. The pod delivered a flagship Observability solution, expanded platform adoption to two new business teams, and achieved full regulatory compliance — while embedding AI-assisted development into the delivery model to lift sprint velocity by ~20% and accelerate flagship delivery. Together, these outcomes establish a scalable foundation for proactive and predictive operations in H2.

---

## H1 / Q2 Key Deliverables & Achievements

**Flagship Delivery — Observability Solution (June Highlight)**
- Designed and delivered an end-to-end Observability platform from the ground up, establishing a centralized intelligence layer and a single source of truth for runtime and SLA performance across calculator operations.
- Introduced a new backend service that functions as a centralized data and intelligence layer — capturing runtime and audit data (start/end times, duration, SLA, status), evaluating SLA compliance in real time, and storing historical data to enable effortless trend and aggregate analysis.
- Exposed simple, reusable APIs that feed dashboards, reporting, and AI tools, turning routine operational activity into actionable, governed insight.
- **Business impact:** ~60–70% reduction in manual monitoring and reporting effort, faster issue identification and resolution cycles, and earlier detection of SLA risks before they affect business users.
- Created a consistent, auditable data foundation that de-risks operations and unlocks the predictive analytics capability on the Q3 roadmap.
- **AI-accelerated delivery:** AI-assisted coding tools compressed the build timeline, enabling the full solution to be delivered in a materially shorter timeframe than a conventional approach would allow.

**Platform Enhancements — Operational Visibility**
- Launched automated AmpliFi dashboards and a standardized Performance Card View, giving business stakeholders self-service, real-time runtime insight without manual reporting overhead.
- Integrated Run1/Run2 comparison capability, improving analysis accuracy and decision confidence for business users.

**Orchestration — Multi-Tenant Scale & Compliance**
- Implemented full multi-tenant support on the ACD Orchestration Platform, enabling new calculators to be onboarded with logical isolation by team — a foundational capability for secure, scalable growth.
- Empowered teams with autonomous, independent release cycles and role-based access control, allowing each team to deploy, promote, and manage only its own DAGs while eliminating cross-team visibility and the risk of accidental interference (e.g., during UAT testing).
- Onboarded two new business teams — LnF and IPV-PnL — expanding platform adoption and embedding the pod's capabilities more deeply into the business.
- Closed 100% of VM15 critical vulnerabilities, achieving full regulatory compliance and materially strengthening the platform's risk posture.

**AI-Driven Productivity & Delivery Acceleration**
- Embedded an AI copilot into the day-to-day engineering workflow, lifting sprint velocity by ~20% — equivalent to recovering roughly one day per developer per working week of capacity.
- Accelerated delivery of the flagship Observability solution, bringing a strategic capability to the business sooner and ahead of a conventional delivery curve.
- Improved test coverage and code quality alongside speed, demonstrating that AI adoption enhances delivery excellence rather than trading quality for pace.
- Established a repeatable, AI-augmented delivery model that scales across future initiatives and compounds productivity gains over time.

**Overall H1 Impact**
- Transformed operations from manual effort to automated, intelligence-driven delivery.
- Improved operational transparency and control for business users.
- Enhanced regulatory standing and platform scalability.
- Proved measurable ROI from AI adoption — faster delivery, higher velocity, and stronger quality — establishing a foundation for continued efficiency gains in H2.

## Q3 2026 Priorities & Outlook

A forward-looking roadmap focused on scaling automation, maturing governance, and transitioning the platform from reactive monitoring to proactive, predictive operations.

**Observability V2 — Next Evolution**
- Strategic SLA redesign: a stage-based SLA framework with an attribution engine to deliver clear ownership and accountability.
- Live SLA breach detection and alerting — shifting from after-the-fact reporting to real-time, proactive intervention.
- Performance optimization through Redis caching and Cairo integration (IPV replacement).
- Enhanced LnF dashboards with Rules Audit integration for deeper governance and traceability.

**Orchestration — Performance & Scale**
- Airflow performance tuning to improve throughput and reliability at scale.
- Correlation service integration (discovery phase) to strengthen cross-system insight.
- Tenant-specific entitlements model and a multi-tenant release framework to enable secure, scalable expansion.

**Regulatory & Platform Modernization**
- PostgreSQL upgrade (v14 → v17) to maintain a modern, supported, and secure technology base.
- Continued VM15 compliance remediation to sustain regulatory standing.

**Support & Stability**
- Ongoing Capital and LiFE platform support.
- Strengthened production incident management and reliability practices.

**Q3 Strategic Themes**
- Transition to proactive and predictive observability.
- Scalable, multi-tenant platform operations.
- Stronger governance and auditability.
- Continued AI-driven efficiency — scaling the AI-augmented delivery model to sustain and grow the ~20% velocity gain across the pod.
